# Music & Audio-Visual Tools

## Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs)

- [Ableton Live](https://www.ableton.com/) - Industry standard for electronic music production and live performance
- [FL Studio](https://www.image-line.com/) - Popular DAW known for its intuitive interface and beat-making capabilities
- [Logic Pro](https://www.apple.com/logic-pro/) - Professional DAW for Mac with extensive virtual instrument library
- [Pro Tools](https://www.avid.com/pro-tools) - Industry standard for professional audio recording and post-production
- [Reaper](https://www.reaper.fm/) - Lightweight and customizable DAW with fair pricing model

- [Processing](https://processing.org/) - Creative coding platform for visual arts
- [p5.js](https://p5js.org/) - JavaScript library for creative coding in the browser
