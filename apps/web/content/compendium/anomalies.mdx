# Scientific Anomalies & Unexplained Phenomena

## Categories of Anomalies

### Physical Anomalies
- Quantum entanglement and non-locality
- Ball lightning and atmospheric phenomena
- Materials with unexpected properties
- Gravitational anomalies

### Biological Anomalies
- Extremophile organisms
- Unexplained animal behavior
- Biological transmutation
- Consciousness studies

### Historical Anomalies
- Out-of-place artifacts (OOPArts)
- Archaeological inconsistencies
- Historical documentation discrepancies
