## Rare Books, Manuscripts, etc

- [ABAA](https://www.abaa.org/) - Antiquarian Booksellers' Association of America
- [ILAB](https://ilab.org/) - International League of Antiquarian Booksellers
- [ALA](https://www.ala.org/membership/ala-personal-membership-benefits-types) - American Library Association
- [ACRL](https://www.ala.org/acrl/membership/join) - Association of College and Research Libraries which has the Rare Books and Manuscripts Section (RBMS). Must join ALA/ACRL to join RBMS.
- [RBMS](https://rbms.info/) - Rare Books and Manuscripts Section of ACRL

## Professional, Computing Societies worth joining

- [ACM](https://www.acm.org/) - Association for Computing Machinery
- [IEEE](https://www.ieee.org/) - Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers
