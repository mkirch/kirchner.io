## Acquiring Good Domain Names

- [ExpiredDomains.net](https://www.expireddomains.net/) - A platform for finding expired domains and learning about the backordering process.
- [OneWordDomains](https://oneworddomains.com) - A resource for finding single-word domain names.
- [Backorder Expired Domains](https://www.expireddomains.net/backorder-expired-domains/) - Information on how to backorder domains that have reached a pending delete state.
