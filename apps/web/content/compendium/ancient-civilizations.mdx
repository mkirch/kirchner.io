# Ancient Civilization

## Timeline of Ancient Civilizations

### [Göbekli Tepe in Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C3%B6bekli_Tepe)   (9500-8000 BC)
- **World's oldest known temple complex**:
  - Sophisticated stone carvings in limestone, terrazzo (burnt lime), and possibly wooden roof beams, including astronomical alignments
  - Discovered in 1963, excavation began in 1995

### [Çatalhöyük in Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%87atalh%C3%B6y%C3%BCk) (7500 BC to 5700 BC)
- **Early urban settlement**:
  - Wall paintings and sculptures
  - Complex social organization
  - Religious practices
  - [Çatalhöyük location](https://geohack.toolforge.org/geohack.php?pagename=Çatalhöyük&params=37_40_00_N_32_49_41_E_type:landmark)

### Sumeria (4500-1750 BC)  
- Language: [Sumerian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sumerian_language), [Akkadian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian_language), [Elamite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elamite_language)
- [Epic of Gilgamesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epic_of_Gilgamesh)
- [Cuneiform writing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cuneiform)
- [Ziggurats](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ziggurat)
- [City-states](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/City-state)
- [Sargon of Akkad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sargon_of_Akkad) (2334-2279 BC)
- [Hammurabi Code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Code_of_Hammurabi) (1792-1750 BC)
